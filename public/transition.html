Transitions Tutorial
Transitions are used to apply time to a pseudo class. This means that if you hover over something, you can make ot take a few seconds to change shape, color, etc.
Example:
Hover over Me
Simply put a transition statement in the CSS for an idea or class, and tell it how long you want each change to take
